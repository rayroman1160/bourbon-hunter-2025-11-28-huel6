Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Bourbon Hunter (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you access or use our website, services, and related tools (collectively, the “Services”).

By using Bourbon Hunter, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Services.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect from and about you when you use our Services, how we use and share that information, and the choices you have regarding your personal data. We are committed to handling your information responsibly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

This policy applies to information collected through our website, applications, communications, and any other interactions you have with Bourbon Hunter. It does not apply to third‑party websites or services that may be linked from our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information collected automatically when you use the Services, and information from third parties where permitted by law.

2.1 Information you provide to us

We may collect information that you choose to provide, including:

  • Account details such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile information and preferences, including your tasting notes, ratings, watchlists, and flavor preferences.
  • Communication content when you contact us for support, feedback, or other inquiries.
  • Any other information you choose to submit through forms, surveys, or other interactions with the Services.

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you access or use the Services, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage data, such as the pages you view, features you use, links you click, search queries, and the dates and times of your visits.
  • Device and technical information, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and similar diagnostic data.
  • Approximate location information derived from your IP address, where permitted by applicable law.

2.3 Information from third parties

Where allowed by law, we may receive information about you from third‑party services and partners, such as analytics providers or authentication services. We use this information to supplement the data we collect and to improve the accuracy and relevance of our Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, or as otherwise described to you at the time of collection:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Services, including personalization of recommendations, tasting insights, and account experiences.
  • To create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support.
  • To analyze usage and performance so we can understand how the Services are used and develop new features and content.
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, administrative messages, and other information related to your use of the Services.
  • To send you marketing communications where permitted by law, and to allow you to participate in surveys, promotions, or events.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our platform, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We may aggregate or de‑identify information so that it can no longer reasonably be linked to an identifiable individual. We may use and share such aggregated or de‑identified data for any lawful purpose.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to help operate and improve the Services, understand user activity, and enhance your experience.

4.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable the site to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

4.2 Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the basic operation and security of the Services.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Services so we can improve functionality and content.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences to provide a more personalized experience.

4.3 Your choices

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies or alert you when cookies are being set. If you choose to disable cookies, some features of the Services may not function properly.

5. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.
  • Law enforcement, regulators, or other parties when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or users.
  • Other parties in connection with a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to appropriate safeguards.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data, or to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can update some of your account information directly within your profile settings. For other requests, please contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request and may decline requests where permitted by law.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

Your continued use of the Services after any changes become effective signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us at:
