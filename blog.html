Getting Started with Our Platform

This guide shows you how to taste bourbon like a pro using a simple, repeatable process. You’ll learn to control your environment, choose the right glassware, and evaluate appearance without overrating color. It explains how to read bourbon aromas using flavor clusters, decode palate, mouthfeel, and finish, and take structured notes that actually inform future purchases. You’ll also learn to build themed tasting flights that rapidly train your palate. Throughout, Bourbon Hunter appears as your digital companion—organizing notes, profiling your preferences, and turning each pour into smarter bottle hunting and more confident bourbon decisions.
